Study done on Feasiblity of the Project

Greater the improper pesticide management, greater the loss in friendly insects that help in improving soil structure and crop productivity. Wrong pesticide can kill those friendly insects, so the famer needs to be fully aware of the pests that are consuming their crops. Only about 11% of the farmer are fully aware of those pests while about 50% are completely unaware. All in all about 80% of the farmer need a better pesticide management approach which can be provided by the system.
